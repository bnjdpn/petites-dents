Petites Dents turns the 20 primary teeth into a simple, reassuring timeline for your family.

INTERACTIVE MOUTH
See upper and lower arches in anatomical order. Every tooth has one clear state: not started, teething, or erupted.

DATES, AGES, AND NOTES
Add your baby's date of birth to see their exact age at each eruption. Tap any tooth to record when teething began, when the tooth erupted, and any observation you want to remember. You can record an eruption directly or reset a tooth at any time.

CHRONOLOGICAL HISTORY
Review erupted teeth in date order, with the baby's exact age, and keep important milestones easy to find.

LOCAL PDF SUMMARY
Create a readable PDF with the full diagram, birth date, eruption ages, dates, and notes. Share it through the system sheet with a pediatrician or keep it with the child's health record.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
No account, application server, advertising, analytics, or tracking. Dental records remain on your device.

The complete app is free. Three optional tips are available for people who want to support its development; they unlock nothing.

Petites Dents is a personal log, not a medical device or diagnostic tool. Contact a qualified healthcare professional if you have concerns about a child's health.
